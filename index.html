Community polls

The community polls are a way to let the community decide how to spend the money donated. By voting, you will have a say in the development process.

Be sure to follow Neovim on Twitter to find out when the polls are announced to help shape the direction of Neovim!

Style guide

The Neovim Community has written a style guide to show the proper form to follow when contributing to the project.

Keeping the code uniform and consistent across the project is important for maintainability. Check out the style guide to see the standards set in place.

Donate

Through Bountysource, you are now able to donate to the Neovim team. All the donations received will go towards the development of Neovim. Any amount donated is very much appreciated by the community.

From time to time, the team will create a bounty for a certain feature or bug with the money donated. If you successfully implement the feature or fix the bug, you'll be rewarded for your efforts.
